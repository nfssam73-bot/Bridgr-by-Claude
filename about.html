The story behind Bridgr.

V

I'm Vishal, a 2nd year MBBS student at BMCRI, and I'm building Bridgr for medical aspirants.

During my own NEET counselling, I didn't have a single person from any college I could actually speak to. Every decision — which college, which city, what to expect — was a guess based on rankings and rumours, not real experience.

"This is to ensure no student regrets having less information about their college."

Bridgr exists so that no aspirant has to go through counselling the way I did — alone, guessing, hoping it works out. It's a platform built by medical students who remember exactly what that confusion felt like, for the students standing where we once stood.

An initiative by doctors, for doctors — and the doctors they're about to become.

Built from experience

Every feature comes from a real gap we faced during our own admissions.
