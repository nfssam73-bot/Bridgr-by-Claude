Frequently Asked

Is Bridgr free?+
Yes — Bridgr is completely free to use during our launch phase.
How are mentors verified?+
Every mentor submits a college ID or admission proof, reviewed manually before approval.
Can I choose my mentor?+
We match you based on your preferences. Requesting a specific mentor may be possible in future versions.
Can I change my mentor?+
Absolutely, yes. If you feel you're not a good fit with your mentor, you can definitely request the admin to change them.
Can I ask multiple doubts?+
Absolutely. Once connected, you can reach out to your mentor whenever you need to.
Can parents join?+
Bridgr is designed around the aspirant, but parents are welcome to be part of the conversation.
How long can I stay connected?+
You can stay connected based on the number of free sessions you have remaining. Paid sessions can continue even after admission — especially if your mentor is from the same college or you've genuinely connected with them — subject to your mentor's availability.
